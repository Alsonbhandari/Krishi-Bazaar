Product Description

Our organic tomatoes are grown using sustainable farming practices without the use of synthetic pesticides or fertilizers. These tomatoes are harvested at peak ripeness to ensure maximum flavor and nutritional value.

Tomatoes are an excellent source of vitamin C, potassium, folate, and vitamin K. They are also a good source of the antioxidant lycopene, which has been linked to many health benefits, including reduced risk of heart disease and cancer.

Growing Conditions

These tomatoes are grown in nutrient-rich soil under optimal conditions. We use natural pest control methods and organic fertilizers to ensure the health of our plants and the quality of our produce.

Harvesting and Handling

Our tomatoes are hand-picked at the perfect stage of ripeness and carefully handled to prevent bruising. They are then sorted, packed, and delivered to you within 24-48 hours of harvesting to ensure maximum freshness.

Storage Recommendations

For best flavor, store tomatoes at room temperature and away from direct sunlight. Refrigeration can diminish their flavor and alter their texture. Use within 5-7 days of delivery for optimal taste and quality.
