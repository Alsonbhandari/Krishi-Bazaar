Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a farmer on Krishi Bazaar?

To register as a farmer, click on the "Join as Farmer" button on our homepage or go to the login/register page and select the farmer option. Fill out the registration form with your details and submit. Once verified, you can start listing your products.

What commission does Krishi Bazaar charge?

Krishi Bazaar charges a small commission of 5% on successful transactions. This helps us maintain the platform and provide quality service to both farmers and retailers.

How are payments processed on the platform?

We offer multiple payment options including UPI, net banking, and credit/debit cards. Payments are held in escrow until the retailer confirms receipt of the products, ensuring security for both parties.

How is delivery handled?

Krishi Bazaar partners with local logistics providers to ensure timely and safe delivery of products. Farmers can choose to arrange their own delivery or use our logistics network at competitive rates.

About Krishi Bazaar

Krishi Bazaar is a platform dedicated to connecting farmers directly with retailers, eliminating middlemen and ensuring better prices for all.
