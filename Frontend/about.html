About Krishi Bazaar

Transforming agricultural commerce in Nepal

Our Story

Krishi Bazaar was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful vision: to revolutionize the agricultural supply chain in India by directly connecting farmers with retailers.

Our founder,Alson Raj Bhandari, a third-generation farmer from Barahakshetra, experienced firsthand the challenges faced by farmers in getting fair prices for their produce. After completing his education in Agricultural Economics, he decided to leverage technology to solve this persistent problem.

What started as a small pilot project in Barahakshetra has now grown into a nationwide platform serving thousands of farmers and retailers across Nepal. Our journey has been one of continuous learning, innovation, and growth, all driven by our commitment to empowering farmers and creating a more efficient agricultural marketplace.

Our Mission

To empower farmers with direct market access and fair prices while providing retailers with quality produce at competitive rates, creating a sustainable and efficient agricultural ecosystem.

Our Vision

To become Nepal's leading agricultural marketplace, transforming the lives of millions of farmers and revolutionizing how agricultural products are bought and sold across the country.

Our Core Values

Integrity
